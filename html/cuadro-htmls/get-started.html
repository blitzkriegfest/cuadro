FAQs

  • Why choose Cuadro?

    Everyone has thousands of photos on their phone, but nobody wants to go through the trouble of going to the photo print shop to have them framed. Seeing photo frames on the table gathering dust is no fun either, but everyone wants to showcase their precious photos. We, at Cuadro solves all these!

  • How do I order Cuadro?

    Visit our website www.cuadro.com and simply upload your favorite photos from your phone or social media accounts like Facebook & Instagram. Then, sit back and we’ll deliver your frames right to your doorstep.

  • What are the sizes of the frames?

    8 x 8 inches or 20 x 20cm. We have 5 beautiful different frames to choose from

  • What is the minimum photo resolution should I use?

    For clear HD-quality photo printouts, your photos should have at least 499x499 pixels or 300 DPI resolution.

  • How much does Cuadro cost?

    Each order begins with ₱ 1,800 for your first 3 frames, then ₱ 450 for each additional frame in your set.

  • Can I hang my Cuadro frames anywhere?

    For best results, make sure that you hang them on clean, smooth, and flat surfaces.

  • How do I hang my Cuadro Frames?

    Your hammer & nails stay in storage where they belong! All of our frames has a tape adhesive on the back side. Just gently strip off the clear tape cover and start creating your wall art.

  • Can I move my Cuadro frame one spot to another?

    Absolutely yes! The special tape adhesive gives you the freedom to move your frames from one spot to another and it will NOT damage your walls.

  • How soon can I receive my photos?

    Upon confirmation of your order, you can expect to receive your beautiful photos within 7-14 days.

  • How much do I pay for shipping?

    Great news! All your orders are FREE of shipping fees, anywhere in the Philippines!

  • Do you ship to other countries?

    Please like our Facebook (https://www.facebook.com/cuadroframes) and Instagram (https://www.instagram.com/cuadroframes/) page for our announcements on this soon!
